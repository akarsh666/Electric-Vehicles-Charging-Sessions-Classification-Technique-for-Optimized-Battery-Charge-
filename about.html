Electric Vehicles Charging

Electric Vehicles Charging refers to the process of replenishing
the battery of an electric vehicle (EV) with electrical energy.
It involves connecting the EV to an electric power source,
typically through a charging station or outlet,
to transfer electricity to the vehicle's battery.

The charging process varies based on the charging infrastructure
and the EV's battery capacity. There are different levels of charging,
such as Level 1 (standard household outlet),
Level 2 (dedicated charging station), and Level 3 (DC fast charging),
each providing varying charging speeds.
